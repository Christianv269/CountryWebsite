Colosseum

The Colosseum was built as a massive free public space for giant crowds to watch and enjoy gladiator fights and showcases, as well as executions around animal fights.

Leaning Tower of Pisa

The leaning tower of Pisa was meant to be straight but due to the softness of the ground it had started to lean over and it was built to showcase the city's wealth and power in the 12th century.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi fountain was built to mark the terminal point of the now restored aqua virgo aqueduct, it also made a nice artistic focal point for Rome's water source. It was built in the 1730s.
