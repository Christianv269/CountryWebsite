Panettone

Panettone is a traditional Italian food more known around the holiday seasons like Christmas and New Years. It is enjoyed as a treat during this times. What it is in fact is simply a sweet dome shaped bread rich with eggs, butter, raisins and candied citrus peel. It origniates from Milan and does take a slow long cooking process to be made and later enjoyed by many people during the most wonderful time of the year.

Pasta

Pasta is a very popular food around the world not just in Italy and it's enjoyed by many. It is made from a simple dough of flour, water, and depending on the situation also eggs, this dough is later rolled out and cut into shapes to form the typical spaghetti, penne and more. It's typically eaten with a top of pasta sauce and cheese on top.
